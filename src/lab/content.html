Fluid Machinery Lab
Welcome to Fluid Machinery Lab
Introduction
Many of our daily life activity depends on fluid machinery. It is important to understand the functioning principles and characteristics of such machinery from an engineering point of view. This lab focuses on giving the user an idea about practical behaviour of such machines before performing operations on a real system.

Experiments
  • Francis Turbine Constant Head
  • Francis Turbine Constant Speed
  • Hydraulic Ram
  • Kaplan Turbine
  • Pelton Turbine Constant Head
  • Pelton Turbine Constant Speed
  • Centrifugal Pump
  • Double Acting Reciprocating Pump


Target Audience

This course is primarily for Sophomore and above students pursuing a Bachelor's program in Civil or Mechanical or related engineering field.

Pre-Requisites

Pre-requisites

This lab is related to a basic course on Fluid Machinery. The required theory for each experiment is given in the page, however, it is recommended that the user goes through a standard material on fluid mechanics and machinery for a thorough understanding.

Software Requirement

This lab uses Javascript and HTML5 for simulations. Please use latest Mozilla Firefox or Google Chrome browser which supports HTML5 with JavaScript enabled for best results.
